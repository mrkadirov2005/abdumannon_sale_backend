Step 1: Create Google Sheet (1 min)

Visit https://sheets.google.com

Create a new spreadsheet and copy the Spreadsheet ID from the URL

https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/YOUR_SPREADSHEET_ID/edit

Step 2: Deploy Apps Script (2 min)

In your Google Sheet, go to: Extensions ‚Üí Apps Script

Copy the code from GoogleAppsScript.gs

Replace YOUR_SPREADSHEET_ID with your actual ID

Click Deploy ‚Üí New Deployment ‚Üí Web app

Step 3: Copy Deployment URL (1 min)

After deployment, you'll see a URL like:

https://script.google.com/macros/d/ABC123/userweb

Copy this entire URL

Step 4: Update Configuration (1 min)

Open: src/config/googleSheetsConfig.ts

Replace the values:

SPREADSHEET_ID: "YOUR_ACTUAL_ID" API_URL: "YOUR_DEPLOYMENT_URL"

Step 5: Restart App (Done!)

Run: npm run dev

Navigate to Finance page

‚ùìCommon Questions

Q: Where do I find my Spreadsheet ID?

In your Google Sheet URL: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/[ID_HERE]/edit

Q: What is the deployment URL?

It's created when you deploy the Apps Script as a Web App. You'll see it after clicking Deploy.

Q: Do I need a database?

No! Google Sheets acts as your database. No additional setup needed.

Q: How long does setup take?

About 5 minutes total. Most time is waiting for Google Sheets to load.

Q: Can I share this with my team?

Yes! Just share your Google Sheet with team members and they can see updates in real-time.
